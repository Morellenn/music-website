Vaporwave

２８１４ - Birth of a New Day

Timeless. A perfect example of what Dreampunk should be, it creates a grossly enveloping atmosphere to get lost and let your mind wander in. Dreamy and ethereal.

Them Are Us Too - Amends

NOT VAPORWAVE - MOVE LATER.
A dreampop/darkwave album with hauntingly angelical vocals coupled with a mix of guitar, synth, and drums. The second half of "Could Deepen" is an especially emotional pick and highlight.
Need to check out their other albums.

エミュレータ - 夢の回想

Ambient at its core. Long and drawn out tracks with elements of nature and lots of drone. Good for focusing. The white noise throughout blends into an "ocean-wave" kind of sound on the songs that focus on the nature element.

Hong Kong Express - 2047

It's by HKE, it's going to be good. First track is a standout with its rhythmic melodies.
Drenched in atmosphere throughout, mostly otherworldly (A Love Story) with one or two songs evoking a more metallic/industrial sound (醉).

Subaeris - The City in Rain

One of the classics. One long track at 57:33 minutes that oozes atmosphere with a light rain effect in the background to provide that core layer of immersion. Unforgettable.

Ken-Ichiro Isoda - Natural Trip Majel's Star

Uses instruments such as the harp, piano and violin to create a Classical Japanese sound, mixed in with raindrops in the background.
Very beautiful throughout.

Infinity Frequencies - Between two worlds

Repitition, pianos, classical music mixed with vaporwave. A classic along with the rest of Infinity Frequencies' albums.
Shut up Luke.

Light Blending In : Last Word

Lots of cold, distant landscapes with some more up-beat ones sprinkled throughout. Great for a late-night listen.
Song 1 (Lamp) is a great, dreamy, atmospheric piece.

t e l e p a t h - Every Time I See Your Eyes

2 long tracks with opposite atmospheres.
The first track is very ethereal and ambient with minimal progression
The second track is a lot more up-beat and rhythmic, akin to something from the 80's Japanese City Pop scene almost.

Nova Dive - Neon Dream

Extremely cold throughout and creates an immersive soundscape to get lost in.
Very reminiscent of some of 2814's best stuff. A hidden gem.
